Privacy Policy for Bloom Village

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Bloom Village is designed as a safe, simple, family-friendly game. This privacy policy explains how the app handles information.

Data Collection

Bloom Village does not collect personal data.

Tracking

Bloom Village does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Advertising

Bloom Village does not show third-party advertisements.

Accounts and Sign-In

Bloom Village does not require account registration, login, username, password, or email sign-in.

Game Progress

Game progress may be saved locally on the device so players can continue their garden. This progress is not sent to a developer server.

Children

Bloom Village is intended to be family-friendly and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app changes. The effective date will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: dotkboy@outlook.com
