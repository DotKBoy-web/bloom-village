Privacy Policy for Bloom Village

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Bloom Village is designed as a safe, simple, family-friendly game.

Data Collection

Bloom Village does not collect personal data.

Tracking

Bloom Village does not track users across apps or websites.

Advertising

Bloom Village does not show third-party ads.

Accounts

Bloom Village does not require account registration or sign-in.

Game Progress

Game progress is saved locally on the device. It is not sent to a server.

Children

Bloom Village is intended to be family-friendly and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the developer through the support page.
