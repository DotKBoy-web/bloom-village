Bloom Village Support

Bloom Village is a simple offline garden game for children and families.

Common help

  • Grow flowers by planting seeds, watering plants, and harvesting blooms.
  • Earn coins by harvesting and selling flowers.
  • Visit the village to buy flowers and furniture.
  • Decorate the house with furniture from the home shop.
  • Use the language button to switch between English and Arabic.
