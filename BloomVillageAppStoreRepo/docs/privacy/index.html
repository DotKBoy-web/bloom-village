Bloom Village Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

Bloom Village is designed as a simple, family-friendly offline garden game.

Data Collection

Bloom Village does not collect personal data from users.

  • No account is required.
  • No name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or identifiers are collected.
  • No analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies are included.
  • No data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Local Game Progress

The app stores game progress locally on the device, such as flowers, coins, furniture, language preference, and garden progress. This information stays on the device and is not sent to the developer or any third party.

Children

Bloom Village is intended to be safe and gentle for children and families. The app does not include ads, in-app purchases, social features, public chat, or user-generated content.

Internet Use

The app is designed to work offline. The support and privacy pages are hosted on the web only so users and Apple App Review can access them.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the developer through the Support page.
