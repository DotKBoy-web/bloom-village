## Description
Bloom Village is a cute and peaceful garden game for children and families.

Grow flowers, water plants, collect blooms, earn shiny coins, and visit a cheerful village filled with baby cats and baby dogs. Players can buy and sell flowers, explore little animal shops, and decorate a cozy garden house with furniture.

The game supports English and Arabic, making it easy for children to enjoy a colourful bilingual experience.

Features:
• Grow and harvest flowers
• Earn stars and coins
• Visit cute animal-owned village shops
• Buy and sell flowers
• Decorate a cozy garden house
• Play in English or Arabic
• Simple, calm, family-friendly gameplay
• No account required
• No ads
• No tracking

Bloom Village is designed as a gentle, safe, and cheerful game experience.
